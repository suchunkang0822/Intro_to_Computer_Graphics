User Instruction

To change your viewing direction, press directional keys as follows

UP: to view upward
Down: to view downard
Left: to view left
Right: to view Rightward

To change position of your camera, press keys as follows

A: to move left
D: to view Right
W: to move forward
S: to view Backward
